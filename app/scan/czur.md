Usage

### Technical Info / Hacking

Temp files are in `%APPDATA%\Roaming\ShineTemp\`. Under this directory:
- `base\`
- `base4imp\`
- `lines\`
- `manual\`
- `sources\`: Original images before processing.
- `thumbnail/`

Seems to clean this dir out on exit?
