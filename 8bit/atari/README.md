### 800XL

Power: DIN-7

    1  +5 VDC   2  Shield   3   GND
    4  +5 VDC               5  GND
    6  +5 VDC               7  GND

Video (marked "Monitor"): DIN-5, NTSC or PAL

    1   Y (luma and composite sync); n/c on PAL 600XL
    2   GND
    3   Audio
    4   CVBS (NTSC or PAL)
    5   C (chroma); only on very late 800XL; PAL 600XL GND; all others n/c
