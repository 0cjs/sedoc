Japanese 8-bit Computing Vocabulary
===================================

Stuff I've encountered while reading Japanese manuals.

    座標    ざひょう        co-ordinate (on a screen)
    領域    りょういき      area (of memory)
    拡張    かくちょう      expansion, extension; maybe "ESC"
    割込    わりこみ        interrupt
    回路    かいろ          circuit
    発生    はっせい        generate
