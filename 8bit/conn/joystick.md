Joystick Connectors
===================

The Atari-ish DE-9 standard ([more platforms here][wp atjoy other]).
Directions and buttons are N.O., shorting to ground when activated.
MSX is compatible but missing button 2.

    Pin Atari   MSX
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
     1  up      up
     2  down    down
     3  left    left
     4  right   right
     5  padl B  Vcc
     6  button  btn 1
     7  +5V     btn 2
     8  GND     strobe out
     9  padl A  GND
