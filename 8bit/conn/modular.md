Modular Connectors
==================

Proper names and common misnamings:

    4P4C    RJ9 RJ10 RJ22       Telephone handset, FM77 keyboard
    6P6C    RJ11 RJ14 RJ25      Telephone

The [FM77AV] uses a 4P4C modular connector for the keyboard.
