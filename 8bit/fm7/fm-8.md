Fujitsu FM-8
============

Switches
--------

From photographs of queuebert's manual, 1-8 are RS-232C and 9-10 are
boot mode.

     9 10
    UP UP   F-BASIC ROM/disk mode
    UP dn   Bubble mode
    dn UP   DOS mode
    dn dn   reserved/unused
