### PC-8801mkII

- Outputs: "B/W" DIN-5, "COLOR" DIN-8; usual pinouts.
- Jumpers under front panel door:
  - `S`,`H`: Probably standard 15 kHz and high 24 kHz resolution.
  - `M`,`T`: ???
  - At `S`,`M` jumpered, monochrome output works on PVM (80 col mode).
