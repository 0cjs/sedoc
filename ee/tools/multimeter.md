Multimeters
===========

Meters:
- Uni-T __UT61E:__ 22000 count, 4-terminal
- Aneng __AN8002:__ 6000 count, 3-terminal
- Elecall __EM33D:__ 2000 count, 3-terminal, usual cheap chipset
