Sizes of Paper, Bags, Boxes, Storage, etc.
==========================================

ISO 216 Paper Sizes
-------------------

- These are also used for ziploc bags and other containers.
- Aspect ratio √2 ≈ 1.414; each smaller size is 1/2 area rounded down.
- B sizes are geometric mean between A sizes.

Table:

         B                 A
    ────────────┬───┬────────────

     728 × 1030 │ 1 │ 594 × 841
